UI/UX Competition Technical Guide

The official guide for all UI/UX competition teams. Please read these provisions carefully.

A. Submission Requirements

Each team must submit the following three main components:

  1. High-Fidelity Figma Prototype Link (View-Only Access).
  2. Presentation Pitch Deck (.PPT or .PDF Format).
  3. Demonstration Video (YouTube/Google Drive Link).

B. Pitch Deck Detailed Guide (Max. 25 Slides)

The pitch deck is a key judging component that must narrate your team's entire process, from idea to solution.

1. Cover & Team Identity

  • Project Title & Team Name.
  • Team Identity: Full names of all members (2-3 people) and their main roles.
  • Chosen Sub-theme and SDGs.

2. Research & Idea Validation

    • User Interview: With 3-5 target audience members.
    • Online Survey: For quantitative data.
    • Competitor Analysis: Analyze 2-3 similar products.
    • Secondary Research: Analyze existing data, articles, or reports.
    • Forum & Review Analysis: Gain insights from app store reviews.
